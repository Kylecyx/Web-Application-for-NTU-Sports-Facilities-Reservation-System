NTU Sport

Swimming Pool



Located next to the Campus Clubhouse, relax and chill at our aquatic facilities. We have a 50m 8-lane Swimming Pool as well as a Children Wading Pool for all recreational activities.


How can I use this facility?

NTU full-time students (including exchange students) and full-time staff can access the swimming pool for free. Please bring along your NTU student/staff card for pool admission. Other categories of users can only use the pool by applying for the NTU Swimming Pool Membership or purchasing a Multiple-Entry Ticket.


Swimming Pool Membership

A membership card for pool admission will be issued upon completion of the application form and payment for the membership fee. Details are available in the Swimming Pool Membership Form, available in the link below.


Multiple-Entry Ticket

You may purchase the ticket at the Sports & Recreation Centre General Office. Priced at $5.00 each (inclusive of GST), it is valid for 6 entries. Ticket holders must be accompanied by NTU student or staff.
