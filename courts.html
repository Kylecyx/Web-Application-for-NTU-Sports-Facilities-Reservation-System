How can I use this facility?

Individual bookings for these facilities must be made via the Online Booking System before use. The system is open to NTU full-time students (including exchange students) and staff.


Other categories of users (including part-time & MOE Bridging students) can only book these facilities after payment of facility fee at the Sports & Recreation Centre General Office.


You can book the following:

  • Badminton hall @ North hill
  • Table Tennis @ North hill
  • Basketball Hall @ SRC
  • Football Field @ SRC
  • Tennis Field @ SRC
