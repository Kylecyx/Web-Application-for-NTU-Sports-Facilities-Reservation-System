North Hill GYM

The North Hill Gym started operation on 3 Jan 2017. Nestled within the North Hill halls of residence, it is also the largest on campus. Besides a wide variety of cardio machines, stack-weight machines and free weights, the gym features Queenax, a state-of-the-art functional training system. It is open to NTU students and staff.


Gym Admission Admission

Admission to North Hill Gym is strictly limited to registered members. Gym membership is free and has to be renewed annually. Please bring along your student matriculation/ staff card for gantry access.


Gym Etiquette

For an enjoyable gym workout, please note the following:
  • 1. Proper exercise attire with covered sports shoes must be worn at all times.

  • 2. A personal towel is to be used on all benches and machines. Sharing of towel is not allowed.

  • 3. Surfaces to be wiped down after use.

  • 4. eturn all equipment to their respective racks after use. No equipment is permitted to be removed from the gym. Theft will be referred to the police.

  • 5. Work out with appropriate strength. Do not throw weights but lower them gently.

  • 6. No food or drink is allowed at the workout areas, with the exception of water and sports drink in a capped plastic bottle. Please clear away any spillage.

  • 7. Portable devices, ie mobile phones, music players, etc should be made inaudible to other users and properly secured to the body of the user while exercising.

  • 8. Do not make and take phone calls on the machine or during workouts.
